Helen Portrey

Actor | Voice Artist | Actor-Musician

About

Helen is an Actor from Stockport, Greater Manchester. She is based across the Greater Manchester and Cheshire area, with an additional base in Scarborough, North Yorkshire.

She trained at CU Scarborough, where she earned a 1st-Class Honours on the BA Acting course in 2022.

After completing the basic Stage Combat at university, Helen discovered a natural aptitude for Stage Combat, and has since gone on to complete several weapons systems at Advanced level. She is working towards the opportunity to complete Specialist-level training in Unarmed.

Helen is experienced in Children's Theatre and as an Actor-Musician. She is a skilled Clarinettist and Pianist, and is confident in her vocal ability as a non-specialist singer.

Her most recent work is with Rusticus Theatre Company, playing The Baker and Jack's Ma in Winter Woodland at Sewerby Hall and Gardens.
