Helen Portrey

Actor | Voice Artist | Actor-Musician

About

Helen is an Actor from Stockport, Greater Manchester. She is based mainly across the Greater Manchester and Cheshire area, but she also has a base in Scarborough, North Yorkshire.

She trained at CU Scarborough, where she earned a 1st-Class Honours Degree on the BA Acting course in 2022.

Helen is experienced in Children's Theatre and as an Actor-Musician. She is a skilled Clarinettist and Pianist. She can play several other instruments to an amateur level, including Ukulele, Recorder, and Kalimba.

Her most recent work is with Rusticus Theatre Company, playing the Baker in Winter Wonderland at Sewerby Hall and Gardens.
